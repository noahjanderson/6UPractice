Fielding Drills
5:00 – 5:25  ·  3 Stations × 8 min ▼
⛰️ Station 1 — Mound: Speed Fielding
  • Charge ground balls, throw to backstop in quick rotation
  • Start from seated position facing center field, charge & throw to backstop
🔁 Station 2 — 1B / 2B: Catch & Throw
  • Player starts at 2B, fields grounder, throws to 1B
  • Player at 2B rotates to 1B after each throw
☁️ Station 3 — Outfield: Pop-Ups
  • Players toss ball straight up to themselves and catch it
  • Goal: toss straight up, not forward — build fly-ball confidence
